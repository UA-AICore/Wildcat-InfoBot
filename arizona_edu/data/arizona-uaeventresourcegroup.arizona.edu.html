Welcome to the University of Arizona Event Resource Group!

The University of Arizona Event Resource Group (ERG), is a growing network of colleagues from all areas of campus that share event planning tools, ideas, resources and protocols. We strive to plan and promote all University events consistently in the tradition and excellence of our institution. 

This site is intended to serve as a resource for university employees who plan, manage and facilitate a variety of events on campus. We continue to evolve, so enjoy your visit and come again! Bear Down!

Contact us at PEUC-ERG@arizona.edu
