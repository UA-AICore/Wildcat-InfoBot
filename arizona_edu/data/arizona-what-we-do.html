Student Legal Services

The Legal Services Office at ASUA provides currently-enrolled UArizona students a one-half hour legal consultation with a licensed State of Arizona attorney free of charge.  

Legal Services is temporary closed.   

After that date, if you need legal assistance please contact the Pima County Bar Association Lawyer Referral Service which offers 30-minute consultations with attorneys for $35.  Here is the link:  Find trusted lawyers - 10000+ clients served | Pima County Bar Association (tucsonlawyers.org)  If you cannot afford to retain an attorney after that consultation you may also try Southern Arizona Legal Aid here: Southern Arizona Legal Aid – Quality Legal Services For All (sazlegalaid.org) or Step Up to Justice here:  Step Up to Justice.  There are also housing resources through Pima County to be found here:  Community - Pima County.

Copies of the Arizona Residential Landlord Tenant Act can be found here:  Arizona Residential Landlord and Tenant Act | Arizona Department of Housing (az.gov)

We do not provide legal representation in a case, that is, we cannot act as your attorney or work on your case long-term.  However, we can give you information about your legal rights and obligations, give advice and referrals, and answer many questions.
