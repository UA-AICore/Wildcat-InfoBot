Annual Security & Fire Safety Report

The University of Arizona prioritizes the personal safety and security of students, faculty, staff and visitors to our campuses.  The Annual Security Reports for each campus are prepared by the University Compliance Office in partnership with the University of Arizona Police Department and with support from multiple departments and offices including the Dean of Students, Housing and Residential Life, the Office of Institutional Equity, Human Resources, Public Safety and Risk Management.  As the only campus with on-campus student housing facilities, the Tucson Main Campus also publishes a combined Fire Safety Report. The purpose of these reports is to fully inform the public concerning important information and to meet the standards outlined in the Clery Act. 

Annual Reports contain statistics for the previous three (3) years of reported crimes that occurred on the University’s main campus and in certain off-campus buildings or property owned, leased, and/or controlled by the University.  University Policies that address safety, security, fire, domestic violence, dating violence, sexual assault, stalking, and alcohol/drug use are also included. 

You may request a hard copy of these reports by contacting cleryact@arizona.edu.

The 2023 Annual Security and Fire Safety Report is comprised of three separate reports. Select the links below to view the report for the Tucson Main Campus, Phoenix Bioscience Core Campus and Sierra Vista CAST Campus.

  • 2023 Main Campus Annual Security & Fire Safety Report
  • 2023 Phoenix Bioscience Core Campus Annual Security Report
  • 2023 Sierra Vista CAST Campus Annual Security Report

For more information on the Jeanne Clery Disclosure of Campus Security and Crime Statistics Act, please visit the US Department of Education.
