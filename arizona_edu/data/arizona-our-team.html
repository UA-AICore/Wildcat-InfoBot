Brand Development

Brand Development

 

The Brand Development team plays a pivotal role in protecting, enhancing and expanding the University brand visibility.  Responsible for Trademarks and Licensing, they safeguard our intellectual property and ensure its legal integrity. The team explores retail opportunities, devising strategies to enhance our presence in the market.  Through strategic partnerships, they contribute to our growth and connection to industry, fostering collaborations across campus that align with the University objectives and values.
